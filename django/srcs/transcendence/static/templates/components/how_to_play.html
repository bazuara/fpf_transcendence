How To Play


General Rules

Use the W and S to move the paddle.

All games are played best of 7, except if a timeout has occurred.

In case of a timeout, if there was a tie, the player on the left wins the game.


Local Multiplayer

Left player uses W and S.

Right player uses Arrow Up and Arrow Down.


2 vs 2 Game Mode

In the 2 vs 2 mode, both players on the same team must press the same key to move their paddle.
